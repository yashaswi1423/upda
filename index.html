Eligibility & Registration

Eligibility

  • Open to undergraduate Engineering Students from recognized institutions.
  • Teams from the same or different departments of the same college are permitted.
  • Each team must comprise 4 to 5 members with at least one female member.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops and necessary peripherals.
  • Each team can register for only one problem statement across the five domains.

Registration

  • Registration is FREE (Maximum of two teams per college are permitted).

Rules and Regulations

General Rules

  • Teams must not switch members after the hackathon begins.
  • All team members must be present throughout the entire hackathon duration.
  • Teams will be disqualified if any team's designated area is found empty without participants.
  • Plagiarism or any form of academic dishonesty will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants are not permitted to leave the campus once the hackathon begins.
  • All decisions by the judging panel and organizing committee are final and binding.

Hackathon Ethics

  • Respect all venue and platform rules and regulations.
  • Maintain ethical collaboration and fair competition practices.
  • No offensive, discriminatory, or illegal content in project submissions.
  • Uphold academic integrity and professional conduct throughout the event.

Requirements

  • Participants are responsible for all hardware and software requirements related to their project.
  • Participants must bring their own laptops and necessary peripherals.
  • Participants are advised to bring all essentials for the 48-hour duration (warm clothing, extra blankets, chargers, prescribed medicines).
  • Participants must carry valid college ID cards and Aadhaar cards for verification.

Conduct and Discipline

  • Participants must maintain professional behavior and cooperate with all team members and organizers.
  • Participants must not damage any property or facilities and maintain proper discipline.
  • Participants must adhere to the dress code (semi-formal attire).
  • Participants must maintain cleanliness and hygiene throughout the campus.
  • The organizing committee reserves the right to disqualify participants for any misconduct.
  • No travel or accommodation allowances will be provided to participants.
  • Alcohol, drugs, narcotics, and any banned substances are strictly prohibited on campus.

Facilities & Support

  • High-speed Wi-Fi and power supply available 24x7
  • Complimentary food and beverages throughout the event
  • Designated rest areas and sleeping facilities
  • Technical mentors and industry experts for guidance
  • Emergency medical support and first aid services
  • Modern computer labs and development environments
